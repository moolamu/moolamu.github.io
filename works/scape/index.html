空景

About _scape

“__scape” is a simple sound installation composed of two pvc water pipes. The audience will experience an unworldly space with spacey sound and specified vision through the water pipes. While what is seen become heard, and what is heard become seen, the audience could shift between two different space at the same pivot point.

《空景》是簡單的聲音裝置，由2條水管組成。我們走進裝置的角度，透過水管，開闊聽覺，進入特定視野，經驗另一空間。然而，在同一點上，我們可以把看到變成聽到的，把聽到變成看到的，在同一點上於兩個對立空間穿插。

The initiative

Stemming from my previous work The Telescope (2012), I want to explore the use of PVC pipe in another format — while The Telescope (2012) has a stunning visual character, appears like a sound sculpture, I want to focus on the listening experience when someone listening closely to the PVC tube.

_scape do not exist on its own — it lives within an environment, and scattered into several points with distance. That's how it's name come from (landscape). It creates spectacular viewpoints from anonymous space. Through the playful and immersive tubes that swapping what you see and what you hear, I wish to create another experience dedicated to a space.

Around Sound Art Festival 2014

“Animoscape” happened on the coastline of Amino, Kyoto. The beautiful coastline of Amino is wide and long, I have 4 sets installed at different points of the beach. One have to spend few minutes to walk through from this here to there, the process of walking is an important element. The mountain foot from the rightmost coastline, the little hill from the leftmost; the electric wires near the house from the back, the lighthouse in a distance; the small invisible island only be seen on a clear day… all live with my work.

《網野空景》發生在日本京都網野的海灘上，是我首件《空景》作品，別具意義。網野海岸廣闊遼長，四件空景裝置分佈於海灘各處，由這邊走到那邊需時幾分鐘，而走路過程本身是構成此作品的重要原素。海岸右邊盡頭的山腳，左邊盡頭的小山丘，後面民居的天線桿，前面遠方的燈塔，最遠處只有在晴天才看得見的小島，都活在作品入面。
